About

Emily is a 22-year-old, hockey & literature lover, and recent college graduate from Saint Joseph's University. She has a B.A. in English, with minors in Communications and Gender Studies. She is dedicated to activism and advocacy for youth in crisis, women, LGBTQIA+ people, and incarcerated people. She is currently living in Chicago, IL.

You can learn more about Emily on her about page.

Work

Emily is currently a youth care worker at Mercy Home for Boys and Girls located in Chicago, IL. She works helps provide therapeutic treatment and advocates for young women ages 11-14.

She formerly worked at Trader Joe's as a crew member and as the Welfare and Accessibility Officer at the Edinburgh University Feminist Society.
