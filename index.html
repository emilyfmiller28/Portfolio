About

Emily is a 21-year-old, hockey and literature lover, and soon-to-be college graduate from Saint Joseph's University in Philadelphia. She has a B.A. in English, with minors in Communications and Gender Studies. In July, she will be moving to Chicago to start a year of service with Mercy Home for Boys and Girls. She is dedicated to activism and advocacy for kids in crisis, women, LGBTQIA+ people, and incarcerated people.

You can learn more about Emily on her about page.
